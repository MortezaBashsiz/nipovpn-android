# Privacy Policy for NipoVPN

Last updated: June 2026

NipoVPN ("we", "our", or "the application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the NipoVPN application.

## Information We Collect

NipoVPN is designed to provide secure network connectivity.

We do not collect, store, or share:

* Browsing history
* DNS queries
* Network traffic contents
* Usernames or passwords
* Personal documents or files
* Payment information

## Device and Diagnostic Information

To improve application stability and performance, NipoVPN may collect limited technical information, including:

* Application version
* Operating system version
* Device model
* Crash reports
* Diagnostic and performance information

This information is used solely for troubleshooting and improving the application.

## VPN and Network Traffic

Network traffic transmitted through NipoVPN is processed only for the purpose of providing the VPN/proxy service.

We do not inspect, record, or sell the contents of your network traffic.

## Third-Party Services

NipoVPN may use third-party services such as:

* Google Play Services
* Firebase Crashlytics (if enabled)
* Firebase Analytics (if enabled)

These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user information to third parties.

Information may only be disclosed when required by applicable law.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information and maintain the security of the application and its infrastructure.

## Children's Privacy

NipoVPN is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published at the same location where this policy is hosted.
